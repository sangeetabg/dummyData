Frequently Asked Questions

What is mental health?

Mental health comprises your social, emotional and psychological well-being. It influences how you think, behave and act. With good mental health, you can take on the stressors of life, while forming social connections with confidence and self-assurance.

What services are provided?

At Heart It Out, we offer services that cater to your specific needs. Be it overcoming anxiety with individual therapy, or resolving relationship conflicts in couple therapy. We have an expert for all! Head over to our “Services” section and pick the service that meets your particular requirement.

How Can Therapy Help?

Therapy can help you find a safe place to vent your feelings and discuss your emotions without fear or judgement. Guided by mental health professionals, you can explore and gain insights that help:

  • Long-term growth
  • In building strategies
  • Conquer disruptive thoughts
  • Regulate complex emotions

    • Who can benefit from therapy?

    Anyone and everyone! Therapy is not only for those suffering from chronic mental disorders. If you want to enhance your emotional and mental well-being under the guidance of a licensed mental health professional, then therapy is for you!

    Who can go to therapy?

    Therapy helps you learn more about yourself, achieve your goals efficiently and form fulfilling social relationships.

  • Craving warmth, safety or a genuine presence?
  • Want to engage in self-growth?
  • Unsure of how to deal with the challenges life throws at you?
  • Want an honest, brainstorming, self-reflective conversation?
  • Want to slow down and explore a different perspective?
  • Don’t feel safe being by yourself?
  • Confused if you need therapy or not?
    • If you answered “yes” to one or more questions on this list then you should really give therapy a chance!

    What to expect from a session?

    Therapy sessions give you a chance to vent out your feelings/emotions in a non-judgemental, safe space. This can be quite a cathartic experience. You feel lighter and less stressed after attending a session with your therapist. You will also have a clearer understanding of your feelings/emotions and how it is affecting the way you think, behave or act in your everyday life.

    Booking your first session

    Heart It Out understands that booking your first session can be a formidable experience. Be guided by our step-by-step “How It Works” section and book your first counselling session smoothly. You can also contact our team of First Responders, who can help you find the right mental health professional and book your session. Reach out to them on +917892551372.

    What if I don’t like it?

    Just like you, therapists have their good days and bad days. If you feel unhappy with our services, feel free to let the therapist know. We appreciate your feedback!
